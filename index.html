DishOut - Welcome to our Home Page

Do you have ingredients gathering dust in your pantry or wetbar? How about something that's about to expire in your fridge? If this is you, then DishOut is the app you've been looking for! Use our food & drink pages to enter those ingredients you've been hoarding and get 5 recipe ideas that use them up. If you're feeling adventurous check out our "House Specials" page to get random drink and food recipes!
